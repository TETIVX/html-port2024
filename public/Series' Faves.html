My Favorite Episodes

This is a list of five of my favorite episodes of the 90's classic Are You Afraid of the Dark?

The Tale of Quick Silver

A creepy episode with a demonic entity living in the walls of a house. The look of the ghost that haunts the house is enough to give you chills for years.

The Tale of The Midnight Madness

A fun a wild ride with the classic vampire Nosferatu. Dr. Vink (Aaron Taggart) makes an appearance in this Season Two thriller.

The Tale of the Dangerous Soup

This episode features a star from a popular movie released two years after this episode aired. The concept is scarier than the execution of it; the part that gets me is when one person has a fear of snakes.

The Tale of the Bookish Babysitter

Belinda the Babysitter is creepy af. The cozy setting of this episode also throws you off; you see that you aren't even comfortable in your own home. The witch from the episode is goofy but disturbing enough. The witch's poor dentation is what really offends.

The Tale of Cutter's Treasure

A classic two-parter that features "Roc (Charles S. Dutton)." 1990s through and through. A wild and fun ride that shows how much brothers mean to each other.
