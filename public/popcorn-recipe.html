Real Deal Movie Popcorn

Ingredients

Corny Bliss

  • 1 cup popcorn kernels
  • 1 tbsp. peanut oil
  • 1 tsp. unrefined coconut oil
  • 1 tsp. water
  • 1-2 tsp. flavored oil
  • liberal sprinkles popcorn salt

Instructions

  1. Bring pot to medium heat.
  2. Add the coconut oil and peanut oil and let melt [few seconds].
  3. Pour in popcorn kernels.
  4. Add the water and cover pot using foil.
  5. Using oven mits, shake a few times throughout cooking process.
  6. Let the kernels pop for about 7 minutes.
  7. Remove from heat, slowly uncover, and top with favorite oil, salt, or flavoring.
